Direct to Garment

DTG uses inkjet technology to print directly onto fabric. It produces photo-quality, full-color prints with no setup cost — perfect for single items or small batches with complex designs.

Best on light or white fabric

Dye Sublimation POPULAR
Sublimation Transfer

Sublimation converts dye into gas that bonds permanently with polyester fibers. The result is a vibrant, edge-to-edge print that never cracks or peels — perfect for all-over prints and sportswear.

What you can print on:
🎽 Sports Jerseys☕ Mugs🖼️ Photo Panels🧢 Caps🏅 Awards
Edge-to-edge (all-over) printing
Never fades, cracks or peels
Photo-realistic quality
Only works on polyester or coated surfaces
From 700 DZD

Ultraviolet Inkjet

UV printers cure ink instantly using ultraviolet light. This allows printing on virtually any rigid surface — glass, metal, wood, plastic — with incredible durability and optionally raised (3D) texture effects.

Commercial Printing

Offset printing transfers ink from a plate to a rubber blanket then onto paper. It's the gold standard for high-volume, high-quality paper printing — used by professional publishers and advertisers worldwide.
